Answer ten quick questions, tell us when you're free, and we'll build two editable weekly workout plans — number of days, time of day, session length, and exercise type — shaped by your dominant Big Five trait.

Built on the BFI-10 (Rammstedt & John, 2007), and on a study of personality & exercise habits in college students, cross-referenced with the broader literature (Rhodes & Smith 2006; Wilson & Dishman 2015; Sutin et al. 2016; Ronca et al. 2025; and others). Every recommendation is a transparent, editable starting point — not a prescription.
